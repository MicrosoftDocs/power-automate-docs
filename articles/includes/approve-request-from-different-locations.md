If you're an approver in an approval flow, you receive an email when someone creates a request. The approval request is also sent to the approvals center. You can then approve or reject requests from the email, the approvals center, or the Power Automate app.

The screens on Android and iOS might differ slightly. The functionality is the same on all devices.

### Approve from email

1. In the email you receive when an item is added to the SharePoint Online list, select **Approve**.

    > [!NOTE]
    > If you're using a mobile device with the Power Automate app installed, the Power Automate app launches; otherwise, the approvals center opens in your browser.

1. Enter a comment, and then select **Confirm**.

### Approve from the approvals center

1. Sign in to [Power Automate](https://make.powerautomate.com).
1. On the navigation pane to the left, select **Approvals**.
1. On the request you want to approve, select **Approve**.
1. At the bottom of the screen, add comments (optional), and then select **Confirm**.

### Approve from Power Automate

1. On your mobile phone with Power Automate installed, select **Approve** from the request approval email.
1. In the upper right corner of the screen, select **Confirm**.

    The success page displays, indicating that your approval was recorded.
